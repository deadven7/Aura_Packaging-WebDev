COUNTERFEITING


Counterfeit drugs are a serious problem;
people can be taking useless drugs or even very dangerous drugs without knowing it.

Customized packaging, special colours, package seals, holograms, and shrink seals/induction seals can be valued parts of an entire security system. 

They help verify that enclosed drugs are what the package says they are.

No packaging system is completely secure, however these security measures go a long way in helping secure popular brands against counterfeiting.
