FOILS


We offer a range of hard Aluminium Foils suitable for packing of Pharmaceutical Products and Food Products.
The final product specifications are made as per requirement of our customers.





Hard Blister Foil


Specifications

  • Thickness -   20/25/30 microns
  • Coating Options -   4-6 GSM / 6-8 GSM
Types

  • Heat Seal Coated for sealing with PVC and PVC/ PVdC
  • Universal Seal Coating for sealing with PVdC/PVC, PP and PS


Applications

  • Suitable for Primary pharmaceutical product packaging such as tablets & capsules and FMCG products like gums & lozenges







Soft Blister Foil


Specifications

  • Thickness -   20/25/30/40 microns
  • Coating Options -   4-6 GSM / 6-8 GSM


Applications

  • Heat Sealable Lacquer is coated on soft foil. Suitable for food packaging such as chocolate and cheese wrappings
