OUTER CARTONS | SHIPPERS




OUTER CARTONS


Outer cartons add additional protection, convenient storage for the retailer, better visibility on the shelf and a bulk pack for discounting or for increasing unit sales.

We can also offer these in quality, reusable plastic boxes with stickers to highlight branding as well as provide statutory information that may need to go with the pack.









SHIPPERS


Size, ply count, bursting strength, edge crush count, paper grammage, etc can be changed as specified of the customer or suggested by us based on customer need / product.

We can also offer plain brown, colour paper or even full colour print shippers for added transit branding and visibility.
This coupled with lamination ensures good visibility with retention over long periods.






Glass container, vials, etc which need extra protection / insulation are customarily sent in large plastic shippers with fastenable lids.
