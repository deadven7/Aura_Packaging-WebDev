CARTONS


Thanks to our comprehensive services, we have the capacity to deliver Pharma Cartons in practically any configuration. Whether you require traditional printing or modern digital options, we do what it takes to deliver.

Options include -

Conventional Offset
UV Offset
Digital
Screen / PVC Screen












E-flute is ideal for high quality printing with a thickness of 1/16″.
B-flute is a good alternative to E-flute when wanting to support heavier items. With a thickness of 1/8″ and shorter flutes, this type of box still provides a rigid, flat surface for printing.
C-flute is commonly used for shippers.

Foil
Inner Printing
Braille / Embossing
