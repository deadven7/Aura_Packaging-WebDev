About Us









We are a consortium of four friends with independent lines in offset, gravure and plastic moulding industries.

With closely coordinated designing and scheduling, we have consistently been able to provide more complete and economical packaging and shipments to our clients for over 12 years now.

Each being experts in our field, we bring a wealth of knowledge and solutions to everyday packaging needs and problems.


All our plants are ISO 9001-2015 certified.
